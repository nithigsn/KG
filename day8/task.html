Tom Cruise

Tom Cruise is an American actor and producer. He is one of the highest-paid actors in the world and has won several awards throughout his career. Cruise is best known for his roles in films such as Top Gun, Mission: Impossible series, Jerry Maguire, and many others.

He was born on July 3, 1962, in Syracuse, New York, USA. Cruise started his career at a young age and rose to prominence in the 1980s.
